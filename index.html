THEME

In Crunchy Crush the theme is Nurture yourself with Nature. This game reinforces the theme because it shows that feeding the crab is necessary or else you lose the game and the crab. My game art style and color scheme reflect on my theme because I wanted to make the screen to grab audience attention and to also to make it fun to play by using candy as a resource for the crab, the crab represents being in nature and getting food. The player will move the crab either left or right to feed the crab, by making it more challenging the candy will fall fast. It's The player versus the candy.

MY WHY

I choose this theme because for me it show appreciation a reminder for nature like a aquatic creature like crab, fish etc.
